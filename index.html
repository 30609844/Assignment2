World Obesity and its causes

This page aims to investigate the causes for rising obesity rates around the world.

As can be seen above, the obesity rate of nations increases with the average wealth of its citizens. However, it is not the sole cause as there are relatively wealthy nations such as Japan and Singapore with a low obesity rate.

A nation's urbanisation rate also seems to somewhat affect obesity rate. However, this trend is seen mainly in developing countries as obesity rates in Western countries such as the USA, UK, and Australia continue to grow despite constant urbanisation rates.

There are other factors that also decide a nations obesity. As can be seen above, nations which consume more fats tend to have higher obesity rates. This is further reinforced by the fact that as more fats are consumed over time, obesity rate tends to increase.
